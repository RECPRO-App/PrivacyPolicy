RECPRO Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RECPRO. We take your privacy seriously. RECPRO is a local utility for recording personal US stock holdings and manually entered OHLC snapshots, memos, and portfolio summaries. Before using the app, please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By tapping "I Agree", you confirm that you have read and accepted this policy.

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. RECPRO does not provide financial advice, trading, brokerage, or live market data.

2. Information We Collect

RECPRO works on your device and does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information from you over the internet. The app may store the following information locally on your device:

US ticker holdings you enter, including share quantity, cost basis, and record dates; OHLC snapshot entries you add manually, including open, high, low, close, volume, and snapshot dates; optional memos attached to holdings or snapshots; portfolio summaries calculated from your local records; and app preferences, such as your privacy consent status.

We do not collect payment information, government ID numbers, precise location data, contacts, or device advertising identifiers for tracking purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use information stored on your device solely to provide and improve RECPRO features, including displaying your holdings list, charts based on your local snapshots, portfolio summaries, search and filtering, and export of your records when you choose to export. We do not sell your personal information or use it for purposes unrelated to the app without your consent.

4. Storage and Protection

Your data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage mechanisms, including the app sandbox and local files managed by RECPRO. Holdings, snapshots, and related records remain on your device until you delete them in the app, reset sample data, clear all data from Settings, or uninstall RECPRO.

We take reasonable measures to protect information on your device. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure. Please protect your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not transmit your holdings or snapshot data to our servers. We may share information only when you have given explicit consent, such as when you export JSON or CSV files and choose where to save or send them; when required by law or a competent authority; or when necessary to protect the legitimate rights and interests of you, us, or other users.

When you first launch the app, it may load this Privacy Policy over the internet. If you tap an external link (such as a mailto link), you may leave the app and be subject to the privacy practices of the destination service.

6. Your Rights

You may export your data as JSON or CSV from Settings; delete individual holdings or snapshots within the app; reset sample data or clear all local data from Settings; and uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device. To ask questions or request assistance, please contact us as described in Section 9.

7. Protection of Minors

RECPRO is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a minor, please read this policy with your guardian and use the app only with their consent. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of RECPRO after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or RECPRO, please contact us at: RECPRO345@outlook.com
